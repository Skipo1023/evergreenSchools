Welcome to Evergreen School

Evergreen Schools draw from the very best of British and Nigerian curriculum content. The curricula empower the child to flow freely with the dynamics of a globalized world. We work with the aim of raising independent thinkers who are open to ideas, inquisitive, confident, kind and responsible. We will groom them to have an enduring love for learning and focus their minds to attaining a purposeful future.

Nuturing global citizens and future game-changers
