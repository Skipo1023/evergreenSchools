Pre School (Creche - Nusery 3)

As a thinking school, the pre school curriculum teaches the children to recognize their self worth.

The pre school cirriculum lays emphasis on learning by observation, interaction and direct guidance.

It incorporates a variety of activities associated with the learning process, dealing various concepts like colors, shapes, numbers and alphabets.

The pupils of this age are conciously provided with expriences geared towards enhanced academic results

The thrust in the curriculum is "learning is fun"
