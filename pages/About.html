We believe that children learn and develop at different rates. It is vital that they are allowed to do so at a pace that suits them. Small class sizes, supportive staff, care for each pupil allows them enjoy an education that is challenging without being overwhelmed.
Our Mission
We shall employ first class resources to provide the best of educational services at the foundation level towards meeting the future needs of our pupils and by so doing become role models for others.
Our Vision
To be the educational cradle of choice in our area of operation.
Our Values
We believe firmly in Christ, hard work, creativity, excellence, responsibility, respect, good manners and love.
Our Curriculum
The curriculum draws on the very best of British and Nigerian curricula with the aim of encouraging independent thinkers. The curriculum says a lot about us. It has been designed to produce excellent leaning outcomes that have an international dimension. Its flexibility means that able pupils can be extended while other pupils can take more time to coordinate learning. Our curriculum illustrates the diversity of pupils and their interest; it expresses our belief that pupils should have breadth to their studies; it reflects our international characters, and it demonstrates that there are worthwhile things to learn outside the conventional set of examined subjects. One of the hallmarks of the school is our small class sizes. The benefits are compelling, pupils find it much easier to speak confidently and freely in small classes and this leads to more productive classroom discussion and make for an optimal teaching and learning experience.
